Community

Whether you have called Old Hill home your entire life or have just moved, we welcome you and are thankful you have chosen this place to call home. Committed to providing beneficial city services we will provide for your needs: superior safety forces, a lively senior center, and fitness programs for all ages at our recreation center.

Our City has always been a place where neighbors know neighbors and come together for the benefit of the community.
