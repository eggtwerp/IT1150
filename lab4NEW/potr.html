Welcome to The Protectorate of the Red Sun!

Old Hill is home for many people that take the saftey of their community into their own hands. This group does just that: keeping our community safe from outsiders since the earliest days of the colonies.

The Protectorate of the Red Sun is older than Old Hill itself, and some say it's older than the mountain to which Old Hill owes its name? No one is quite sure of it's origins, but one thing's quite clear. The Mountain is older than God! The P.o.t.R.S. is not currently accepting members under the age of 55. Thank you for your understanding.
